About Us

Grand River Analytics is a student-run financial publishing website focused on showcasing qualitative techniques through equity reports. We aim to present clear, defensible analysis that’s easy to audit.

The name Grand River Analytics comes from the Grand River — a river that flows through both the universities of Waterloo and Laurier, where members of our team study.
